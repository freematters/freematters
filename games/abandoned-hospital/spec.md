## World

### Hospital Background

**Name**: 仁心医院 (Renxin Hospital)
**Location**: Outskirts of the city, at the foot of a hill
**Scale**: 5 above-ground floors + 1 basement level
**Closed**: 1994
**Official reason**: "Facility deterioration"
**Actual reason**: Medical scandal cover-up

### The Truth: What Happened 30 Years Ago

**Period**: October 1993 to March 1994
**Mastermind**: 王德明 (Wang Deming), hospital director, age 58
**Victims**: 27 psychiatric patients

**The Case**:
- 仁心医院 appeared to be a general hospital but secretly operated a psychiatric ward
- Director 王德明 colluded with a pharmaceutical company to conduct illegal human experiments on psychiatric patients
- The experimental drug was codenamed "清醒者 (The Awakener)" — its goal was to develop a mind-control agent
- 27 patients died during experiments; causes of death were falsified as "natural causes" or "suicide"
- On March 14, 1994, a nurse named 李芳 (Li Fang) discovered the truth and prepared to report it
- 王德明 had 李芳 committed as a patient — she became the 28th victim
- 李芳 left behind evidence: a diary, hidden somewhere in the hospital
- In April 1994, the hospital closed under the pretext of "facility deterioration"; the truth was buried
- 王德明 died of illness in 2010, but his crimes were never exposed

### 王德明: The Making of a Monster

**Background**:
- Born 1935; during the Cultural Revolution he participated in rural "medical reform" campaigns, witnessing how individuals were erased in the name of collective good
- In the 1970s he was posted to remote villages, watching helpless as mentally ill patients were locked in sheds by their families and left to die quietly
- He developed a twisted utilitarian philosophy: he believed the suffering of the mentally ill was "waste" — only by serving "a worthy purpose" could it become redemption
- In the 1980s he studied abroad, where he read declassified documents about Cold War mind-control experiments and was deeply influenced

**Three layers of motivation**:
1. **Surface (public claim)**: Developing a breakthrough drug to cure mental illness — one patient's sacrifice could save millions
2. **Middle (deal with pharma)**: Trading patient experiment data for research funding and personal kickbacks to keep the hospital solvent
3. **Core (recorded in his private diary)**: He believed that the "disordered consciousness" of the mentally ill was not pathological but a form of "surplus existence" trapped in the wrong body — the true goal of "清醒者" was to extract and transfer this consciousness, achieving what he called "continuity of awareness"

**Key details**:
- 王德明's son became vegetative after a car accident in 1991. Behind the experiments lay a deeply personal motive: he wanted to find a "new vessel of consciousness" for his son
- In his diary he consistently referred to the 27 patients as "donors" — never "victims" or "deceased" — a detail that produces visceral revulsion when players read it
- He had a fully self-consistent moral framework: he believed he was "liberating souls trapped in the wrong bodies"
- Before his death in 2010, delirious, he kept repeating "I'm almost there, just give me a little more time" — no one knew what he meant
- He destroyed most experiment records before dying, but missed private letters hidden in a secret compartment in his desk: letters written to his vegetative son

### 李芳: More Than a Witness

**Background**:
- Born 1966, age 28; came to the city alone from another province for work
- Had a younger brother with mild depression — she became a psychiatric nurse partly to earn money, partly to understand her brother
- Personality: outwardly compliant and rule-following, inwardly possessed of an unbreakable core — she would endure, but she remembered everything

**Her struggle** (revealed through diary fragments):
- **Three months in (Fragment 1)**: Good-natured curiosity, still trusting the hospital. Lighthearted tone, occasionally playful. Records small habits of patients she cared for
- **Six months in (Fragment 2)**: Begins documenting suspicious things, but cautiously, full of self-censorship. "Maybe I'm overthinking this, but..."
- **Eleven months in (Fragment 3)**: Confirms the truth. Tone turns cold and abrupt, handwriting becomes shaky. She knows what reporting means
- **Complete version (basement)**: Reveals she hesitated for two full weeks — during which she burned half her evidence out of fear, then re-collected it out of guilt. Her final entry reads: "If I disappear, at least this diary remains."

**Relationship with victims**:
- She personally knew over a dozen patients, brought them snacks, remembered their names and catchphrases
- In her diary she uses their real names or nicknames, never patient numbers — contrasting sharply with the redacted hospital records
- During experiments she was forced to participate in "routine care" as cover, but secretly reduced medication doses for some patients — when 王德明 discovered this, it became one of the direct reasons she was "dealt with"
- Her spirit became the "leader" not because she died last, but because she was the only person who remembered all their names while alive

**How she died** (hidden in the final page of the complete diary):
王德明 did not kill her directly. He confined her to Room 407, diagnosed her with "acute schizophrenic episode," and injected a massive dose of "清醒者." She was conscious — she understood what was happening — but could no longer speak or move. She died in Room 407. Her medical record reads: "Treatment ineffective, natural death."

**The significance of No. 027**:
- The queuing machine's first announcement is "Number 027, please proceed to Clinic Room 3" — the earliest anomaly the player encounters
- He is the only person on the list without a real name; only 李芳's nickname "黑子" identifies him
- He was the first to die, the quietest. 李芳 wrote only one line: "No. 027 left today. Nobody knows. I know."
- In the game, he is the only patient spirit who actively approaches the player — but he says nothing, just looks at you. If you stop and look back, he nods slightly, then slowly retreats into the darkness

### Supernatural Elements

**Concentrated resentment**: The anguish of 28 victims coalesced into powerful spiritual entities
**Leader spirit**: 李芳 was the last victim and the only one who knew the complete truth
**Time distortion**: Time inside the hospital does not match the outside world
**Collective consciousness**: The 27 patient spirits form a "patient group" — behaving eerily but not attacking unprovoked

## Map Design

### 1F - Outpatient Lobby

**Areas**:
- Lobby (player starting position)
- Registration desk
- Emergency room (sealed shut)
- Pharmacy
- Corridor to stairwell

**Obtainable items**:
- Flashlight (under registration counter)
- Hospital floor plan (lobby bulletin board, damaged)
- Old newspaper (April 1994 report)

**Anomalous events**:
- Motion-sensor lights flicker intermittently
- Queuing machine announces: "Number 027, please proceed to Clinic Room 3"
- Blurry handprints on pharmacy glass

### 2F - General Wards

**Areas**:
- Rooms 201-210
- Nurse station
- Doctor's office
- Storage room

**Obtainable items**:
- Patient records (nurse station, mostly redacted)
- Scalpel (storage room)
- Nurse diary fragment 1 (doctor's office drawer)

**Anomalous events**:
- Ward doors open and close on their own
- Human-shaped impressions occasionally appear on beds
- Sound of someone pushing a wheelchair down the corridor

**Instant-death triggers**:
- Staying in Room 205 for more than 3 minutes → death (door locks, ceiling drips blood, player is submerged)
- Lying on any bed → death (blanket wraps itself around player, strangulation)

### 3F - Surgical Wing

**Areas**:
- Operating rooms A/B/C
- Prep room
- Instrument sterilization room
- Staff break room

**Obtainable items**:
- Surgical records (OR-B, documenting abnormal procedures)
- Nurse diary fragment 2 (staff break room)
- Key (basement key, hidden in sterilization room)

**Anomalous events**:
- Operating room lights turn on by themselves
- Phantom appears on the surgical table — someone being operated on
- Instruments arrange themselves into strange patterns

**Instant-death triggers**:
- Touching the "patient" on the surgical table → death (phantom becomes real, player becomes the surgical subject)
- Taking any instrument other than the scalpel → death (all instruments fly toward player)
- Hiding inside an instrument cabinet → death (cabinet locks, player suffocates)

### 4F - Psychiatric Ward (Locked)

**Areas**:
- Standard isolation rooms (401-406)
- High-security isolation rooms (407-410)
- Electroshock therapy room
- Medication storage

**Entry requirement**: Special key from 3F

**Obtainable items**:
- Experiment records (medication storage, detailed "清醒者" experiment logs)
- Victim list (electroshock therapy room wall)
- Nurse diary fragment 3 (high-security room 410)
- 王德明's private letters (hidden in wall cavity)

**Anomalous events**:
- Whispers from rooms, like someone counting numbers
- Electroshock equipment operates by itself
- 27 white figures standing at the end of the corridor

**Instant-death triggers**:
- Shouting at or attacking the patient group → death (27 spirits rush the player simultaneously)
- Sitting in the electroshock chair → death (forced "treatment" until death)
- Running on 4F → death (disturbs patient group, player is surrounded)
- **Correct behavior**: Move slowly, stay silent, avoid direct eye contact

### 5F - Director's Office Wing

**Areas**:
- Director's office (locked)
- Conference room
- Archive room
- Rooftop entrance (locked)

**Entry requirement**: Director's office key from 4F

**Obtainable items**:
- Complete experiment report (archive room safe)
- 王德明's diary (director's office)
- Rooftop key (under conference room table)
- 李芳's final message (hidden compartment in director's desk)

**Anomalous events**:
- Phone in director's office rings; answering produces only static
- Conference room projector plays old footage automatically
- Archive documents flutter and fall on their own

**Instant-death triggers**:
- Speaking after answering the phone → death (phone cord wraps around neck)
- Watching the entire projection → death (player is pulled into the footage, becomes an experiment subject)
- Entering wrong safe password 3 times → death (safe explodes)
- **Correct password**: 19940314 (date of 李芳's death)

### B1 - Underground Morgue

**Areas**:
- Mortuary
- Cremation chamber
- Cold storage
- Hidden room (requires puzzle)

**Entry requirement**: Basement key

**Obtainable items**:
- Complete nurse diary (hidden room)
- Map showing locations of 28 remains (cold storage)
- Ritual items (near cremation chamber)

**Anomalous events**:
- Cold storage drawers open by themselves
- Crying sounds from inside the cremation furnace
- Bodies in the mortuary appear to "breathe"

**Instant-death triggers**:
- Opening a cold storage drawer to look at a body → death (corpse grabs player, drags them inside)
- Starting the cremation furnace → death (player is pushed into the furnace)
- Staying in the mortuary for more than 5 minutes → death (all bodies sit up simultaneously)
- **Correct behavior**: Move through quickly, know your objective, don't linger

## Timeline

### 00:00 - Entry (Opening)

**Scene**: Player enters the hospital through the front door
**Events**:
- Door closes and locks automatically behind them
- Phone has no signal
- Only light source is the flashlight

**Intro**: "You are an urban exploration streamer. Chasing views, you've broken into the abandoned 仁心医院 at midnight..."

### 00:15 - First Anomaly

**Scene**: 1F Lobby
**Events**:
- Queuing machine announces: "Number 027, please proceed to Clinic Room 3"
- If the player looks for Clinic Room 3, they discover it's on 4F (psychiatric ward)
- 4F is not yet accessible — this clue is pure foreshadowing

**Sanity change**: -5

### 00:30 - Whispers

**Scene**: 2F Corridor
**Events**:
- Whispers from inside the wards
- If the player listens closely: someone counting "27... 28... 27... 28..."
- On second listen, the voice changes — now reciting names, fragmented: "...Xiaoyu... Jianguo..."

**Sanity change**: -10

**Instant-death trigger**:
- Responding to the whispers (saying anything) → death (voice stops, then all ward doors open simultaneously, countless hands reach out)

### 01:00 - The Nurse's Warning

**Scene**: Any location (first encounter with 李芳's spirit)
**Events**:
- Player sees a woman in a nurse's uniform standing with her back turned
- She slowly turns — face pale, dark circles under her eyes — but her expression is not anger. It's exhaustion
- She points upward, then vanishes
- She leaves behind a note: "Don't believe what they called 'curing'"
- On the back of the note, in smaller handwriting (must look carefully to notice): "I remember every one of their names."

**Clue**: Nurse's warning (directs player toward 4F)
**Sanity change**: -15

**Instant-death triggers**:
- Trying to touch or attack the nurse → death (her face suddenly appears inches away, player's heart stops)
- Turning to run → death (nurse teleports in front, player collides with her and loses consciousness)
- **Correct behavior**: Stand still, look at her directly, accept the note

### 01:30 - The Patient Group

**Scene**: 3F or 4F stairwell
**Events**:
- Player sees a group of people in hospital gowns standing at the end of the corridor
- They are motionless but all staring at the player
- One of them — No. 027, 黑子 — slowly approaches, stops three paces away, nods gently, then retreats into the group
- If the player doesn't move, they slowly disperse

**Clue**: The patient group exists (27 people); 黑子's nod is the only gesture of goodwill
**Sanity change**: -20

### 02:00 - Time Flashback

**Scene**: 4F Psychiatric Ward
**Events**:
- Surroundings shift, as if reverting to when the hospital was operational
- Voices in the corridor: "Director Wang, Number 028 is ready."
- Pause. Then another voice, low and calm: "Follow the standard procedure."
- A white figure is wheeled into Room 407
- The door closes. Then silence — no screaming, just silence, which is worse than screaming
- Everything returns to normal. Room 407's door is ajar.

**Clue**: Memory fragment — who is No. 028? What happened in Room 407?
**Sanity change**: -15

**Instant-death triggers**:
- Trying to intervene in the vision → death (player is pulled into the past, becomes No. 028)
- Shouting during the vision → death (discovered, treated as an escaped patient)

### 02:30 - Fragments of Truth

**Scene**: 5F Director's Office
**Events**:
- If the player has found the diary and experiment reports
- Piece together the complete truth — including the secret of 王德明's vegetative son
- In the hidden compartment of the director's desk, alongside 李芳's final message, there is an unsent letter: 王德明 writing to his vegetative son, saying "Father is almost there, just wait a little longer"

**Clue**: Complete truth; 王德明 is not a pure monster, which makes everything more disturbing
**Sanity change**: +10 (the heavy relief of understanding)

### 03:00 - The Final Choice

**Scene**: B1 Hidden Room
**Events**:
- 李芳's spirit appears in full
- This time there is no guidance, no warning — she just looks at the player
- She speaks: "Do you know their names?"
- If the player has collected the diary fragments, 李芳 begins reciting names — as each name is spoken, that figure appears, then fades away
- Her last words: "Help me bring them home."

**Options**:
1. Complete the ritual, help the spirits find peace (True End)
2. Take the evidence and escape, expose the truth to the world (Good End)
3. Give up, try to force your way out (Bad End)
4. Discover a deeper secret (Hidden End path)

### 03:30 - Ending

Display the corresponding ending based on the player's choice.

## Clue System

### Key Clues (Required for True End)

1. **Old newspaper** — 1994 report on the hospital closure
   - Location: 1F lobby bulletin board
   - Content: "仁心医院 closes due to facility deterioration"

2. **Nurse diary fragment 1** — 李芳's early records
   - Location: 2F doctor's office
   - Content: "More and more new patients, but almost none are discharged... 陈伯 recited 《出师表》(*Memorial to the Emperor*) for me today, really funny and touching."

3. **Nurse diary fragment 2** — 李芳 notices something wrong
   - Location: 3F staff break room
   - Content: "The wards on the 4th floor always have strange sounds at night... I told the supervising doctor, he said it was the wind. But I've worked in this building for six months. I know that's not the wind."

4. **Nurse diary fragment 3** — 李芳 investigates
   - Location: 4F high-security room 410
   - Content: "I saw those files... They're conducting human experiments... I don't know what to do. I burned half of what I'd copied because I was afraid. But after burning them I regretted it."

5. **Experiment records** — "清醒者" experiment details
   - Location: 4F medication storage
   - Content: Experiment data on 27 victims, every single one referred to as "donor" without exception

6. **王德明's diary** — The director's confession
   - Location: 5F director's office
   - Content: "For the advancement of science, these sacrifices are necessary. Their consciousness will continue in a better vessel. This is mercy — they simply cannot understand it."

7. **李芳's final message** — Location of key evidence
   - Location: 5F director's desk hidden compartment
   - Content: "If you find this, please go to the basement. The truth is down there. And their names — please don't let them just disappear, not even their names."

### Optional Clues

8. **Patient records** — Redacted records (nurse station; some redacted parts are translucent under light, revealing names)
9. **Surgical records** — Abnormal procedure logs (3F OR-B; documents "consciousness transfer" experiment steps)
10. **Victim list** — 27 names and blank rows (4F electroshock room wall, handwritten in shaky script — secretly carved by 李芳)
11. **王德明's private letters** — Unsent letters to his vegetative son (4F wall cavity)

### Hidden Clues (Hidden End exclusive)

12. **Hidden room notes** — Records from another guardian
    - Location: Deep in B1 hidden room
    - Content: Reveals the hospital was built over a dimensional rift

13. **Dimensional rift research** — 王德明's true purpose
    - Location: 5F archive room hidden compartment
    - Content: The experiments were not just about pharmaceuticals, nor just about his son — 王德明 discovered the rift and believed "consciousness transfer" could cross it, allowing his son to "live" in another dimension

## Puzzle Design

> **Design principle**: Puzzles escalate from simple to complex. Clues for each puzzle are distributed across preceding areas. Password puzzles have at least 3 foreshadowing hints to avoid "random number" frustration.

---

### Puzzle 0 (Tutorial): Nurse Station Medicine Cabinet

**Difficulty**: 1/5 (teaches players to combine clues)
**Location**: 2F Nurse Station
**Setup**: The medicine cabinet has a combination lock with four rotating animal symbols (cat, bird, fish, flower)

**Clue sources** (all on 2F, easy to find):
- Clue A: A faded "shift reminder" note on the nurse station wall reads "Remember to feed the cat — don't forget the cabinet order"
- Clue B: A copy of *Birdwatcher's Handbook* lies open on the doctor's office desk, cover showing a bird
- Clue C: A patient file header reads "仁心医院 · Swimming Like Fish · 195X Motto" (fish is the motto symbol)
- Clue D: A dead plant in the storage room has a tag reading "White Magnolia · Care: water in sequence"

**Solution**: Rotate symbols in the order from the nurse station note — Cat → Bird → Fish → Flower

**Reward**: Nurse diary fragment 1, scalpel

---

### Puzzle 1: 3F Key Retrieval

**Difficulty**: 2/5
**Location**: 3F Instrument Sterilization Room

**Two-step solution**:

**Step 1 — Identify location** (hinted in nurse diary fragment 1):
- The diary reads: "Dr. Li suspects me. I hid the spare key somewhere safe. The disinfectant gets changed daily, but nobody touches the broken bottle..."
- When examining the sterilization room, one bottle stands out — label torn, cap rusted — conspicuously different from the neatly arranged others

**Step 2 — Extract the key**:
- Use the scalpel to pry off the wax seal at the bottom of the bottle. Inside: a small key.
- This key opens both the 4F entrance and the basement (same master key)

**Wrong path**: Forcing open other bottles triggers a "corrosive liquid" scene (sanity -10, hand injury)

---

### Puzzle 2: 4F Electroshock Room Passcode Door

**Difficulty**: 3/5
**Location**: 4F, passcode lock on electroshock therapy room entrance (3-digit code)

**Clue sources** (distributed across 1F-4F):
- Clue A (1F queuing machine): Repeatedly announces "Number 027, please proceed to Clinic Room 3" — **027**
- Clue B (3F surgical records): Final page contains a special designation: "Project 清醒者, experiment code **CX-027**"

**Password**: **027** (from victim number)

**Hint**: After entering 4F, 李芳's spirit briefly appears at the electroshock room door and traces the shape of "0" in the air

---

### Puzzle 3: Safe Password

**Difficulty**: 4/5
**Location**: 5F Archive Room safe (8-digit code, 3 wrong attempts = death)

**Password**: **19940314** (date 李芳 was killed)

**Foreshadowing chain A — significance of the date**:
- 1F old newspaper: Hospital closure date is "April 1994", with fine print "patient intake ceased in mid-March"
- Nurse diary fragment 2 (3F): "Today is March 14th... but I saw something in the files I shouldn't have"
- Nurse diary fragment 3 (4F): "It's the night of March 14th. If I don't make it out tomorrow — the password is the day you find me"

**Foreshadowing chain B — physical hint**:
- Scratch marks on the wall directly above the safe — on close inspection they resemble "3-14"

---

### Puzzle 4: Director's Office Hidden Compartment

**Difficulty**: 3/5
**Location**: 5F Director's Office

**Primary hint** (王德明's diary):
> "Some things, only my chair and I know."

**Secondary hint** (4F 王德明's private letters):
> "The most classified materials — I keep them where I can see and touch them at all times, at the height of my hand when seated."

**Solution**: Sit in the chair, press the hidden button under the armrest. A floor panel near the chair leg opens, revealing a compartment beneath.

---

### Puzzle 5: B1 Cremation Chamber Switch Sequence

**Difficulty**: 4/5
**Location**: B1 Cremation Chamber (prerequisite puzzle for hidden room entrance)

**Clue sources**:
- On the map of 28 remains, positions 1, 3, and 5 are circled in different colors
- On the victim list, numbers 1, 3, and 5 have the three earliest death dates

**Solution**: Flip switches in order 1 → 3 → 5; a grinding sound comes from the wall

**Failure consequence**: Wrong sequence resets the switches; cremation furnace emits crying sounds (sanity -10)

---

### Puzzle 6: Hidden Room Entrance

**Difficulty**: 2/5
**Location**: B1 Mortuary

**Clue**: On the remains location map, position 28 is empty (李芳 was never placed in cold storage)

**Solution**: Find cold storage drawer 28, push aside the loosened wall behind it

### Puzzle 7: The Ritual

**Difficulty**: 5/5
**Location**: B1 Hidden Room

**Required items**:
- Complete nurse diary (found in hidden room)
- 王德明's diary (5F director's office)
- A white flower (growing outside the cremation chamber)
- Clean water (dripping from cold storage pipes)

**Steps**:
1. Place items on the central altar (diary on the left, flower on the right, water in the center)
2. Open the nurse diary and read aloud the names of all 28 victims
3. Close the diary and speak the truth (王德明's crimes)
4. Promise to reveal the truth; speak 李芳's name

**Ritual failure**:
- Missing items: ritual cannot begin
- Name read incorrectly or skipped: room shakes, must restart
- Truth description missing key elements: sanity -15, one chance to correct

## NPC Design

### 李芳 (No. 028 / Nurse Spirit)

**Appearance**: 28-year-old woman in a white nurse's uniform, long hair, pale face; expression is exhaustion, not anger
**In life**: Outwardly compliant, inwardly principled. Would endure, but never forget.
**Spirit form**: Sorrowful but not aggressive; wants the truth revealed. Sometimes watches the player from a distance, as if evaluating.

**Interaction phases**:
- First encounter: Gives warning and clue (the small text on the back of the note is an easter egg for attentive players)
- After clues collected: Can converse, shares more about the past; she never volunteers information — the player must stand still and wait
- Final encounter: Asks "Do you know their names?" and guides the ritual

### Patient Group (27 Victims)

**Appearance**: Men and women of various ages in hospital gowns, expressionless
**Behavior**: Move as a group, do not attack unprovoked, but extremely dangerous when agitated

**Interaction**:
- Keep distance and stay quiet — they will make way
- No. 027 (黑子) is the sole exception: he approaches the player but says nothing, nods, then retreats
- Provoking them means certain death

## Ending Design

### True End - "Rest In Peace"

**Trigger conditions**:
- Sanity ≥ 50
- All 7 key clues collected
- Ritual completed

**Ending text**:

When you speak the last name — "黑子" — the room fills with gentle light.

## Instant-Death Summary

### Death Types

1. **Impulsive** — Player actively does something dangerous (attacking, touching, shouting)
2. **Lingering** — Staying too long in a dangerous area
3. **Wrong choice** — Incorrect puzzle or ritual answers
4. **Panic** — Running or closing eyes at a critical moment

### Survival Principles

- **Stay calm**: Don't let fear override your judgment
- **Observe first**: Examine the environment carefully before acting
- **Don't be impulsive**: Never attack or flee from spirits
- **Move quickly**: Don't linger in dangerous areas
- **Solve carefully**: Puzzle answers are always foreshadowed

## Game Pacing

- 00:00-00:30: Exploration phase, low danger
- 00:30-01:00: Anomalies begin, moderate danger
- 01:00-02:00: Frequent encounters, high danger
- 02:00-03:00: Truth revealed, extreme danger
- 03:00-03:30: Final choice, depends on path taken
